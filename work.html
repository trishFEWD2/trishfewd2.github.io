A Typical Website: Summary of workflow

  • Discovery

    The first phase of your project is to gather information about you, your business, competitors, goals, and more. The more information you can supply, the better.

  • Content

    You know your business best and are the expert in your field. I can help you polish your writing and assist with image selections. Professionally shot photos of your products or services are the first choice if it's within your budget.

  • UX/UI

    User personas, existing or competitor website analysis, site maps, and wireframes are developed before the final prototyping of the site begins. After you approve the final design and interactions the website development process begins.

  • Coding

    My understanding of HTML, CSS, and Javascript allows me to create your website. To save money and time, websites can look great with existing WordPress themes. If necessary I can work with specialized developers for complex custom websites.

  • Roadmap

    I help you map out a social media and search engine optimization strategy. Email marketing is still an effective sales tool that I can design and plan for you.

  • Follow up

    As your business grows, I can help you with your next phase. You may need signage for your new space, a booth designed for a trade show, or a cookbook design after your successful restaurant opening. Business cards and letterhead
